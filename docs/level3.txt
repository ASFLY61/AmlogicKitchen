Kitchen for working with the third level of Amlogic firmware
-------------------------------------------------- ------

Instruction:

1) to work with the third level, you must unpack level 1 (see level1.txt)

2) click _unpack_level3.bat

3) in the level3 folder, we replace / correct the files in the folders:

boot - boot partition (kernel)
recovery - partition recovery
logo - logo section
devtree - devicetree section

4) click _pack_level3.bat

5) then pack level 1 (press _pack_level1.bat, see level1.txt)
    to create full OTA, click _pack_level1_fota.bat

6) enjoy.
